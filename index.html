Welcome to New York City

We're compiling and connecting open data sets from and about NYC government to make it easier for you to understand what city agencies are doing and how they're spending money.

Here some things you can do on this website:

* Browse hundreds of city agencies and other government departments relevant to New Yorkers.

* Filter and search through the services offered by city agencies (via Benefits and Programs API)

* Filter and search through  the capital projects of city agencies (via Capital Commitment Scrape)

* See the names and contact information for hundreds of city employees (via Greenbook)

This website is a work in progress. We're constantly adding and updating data and improving our software.

Learn more about our project and how to get involved!
